Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

Cardiac Mass DSS ("the App") is a clinical decision support tool designed to assist cardiologists in assessing cardiac masses. This application processes all data locally on your device and does not transmit any patient or clinical information to external servers.

2. Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, patient data, or health information to any server or third party. All clinical data entered by the user is stored exclusively on the device using the browser's localStorage mechanism and never leaves the device.

3. Data Storage

Case data (imaging findings, risk assessments) is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by clearing the app's local storage or uninstalling the app.

4. Medical Disclaimer

This application is intended as a research and educational tool to support clinical reasoning. It is not a certified medical device and must not be used as the sole basis for clinical decisions. Always apply professional clinical judgement.

5. No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not use any analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking technologies.

6. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the development team at the University of Bologna.
